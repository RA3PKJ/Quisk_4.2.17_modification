Types of radio

Odyssey - DDC/DUC transceiver, audio codec on board, hardware CW, IP 192.168.2.160 .
RX2 - this is to launch the secondary Quisk for the second receiver in Odyssey, IP 192.168.2.161 .
HiQSDR - DDC/DUC transceiver HiQSDR or HiQSDR-mini, IP 192.168.2.196 .
srusb - Softrock USB transceiver with synthesizer Si570, VID 0x16C0 and PID 0x05DC .
Hermes - DDC/DUC transceiver Hermes-Lite.
SDR-IQ - USB-COM DDC receiver.
Red Pitaya - DDC/DUC transceiver Red Pitaya, IP 192.168.0.15/24 .
SoftRock - SoftRock receiver or transceiver with quartz.
